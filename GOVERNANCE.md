# Governance

## Project Leadership

Orion is currently maintained under a **Benevolent Dictator for Life (BDFL)** model. The original author, maintainer, and lead architect retains final decision-making authority over the project's direction, design, and releases.

This ensures consistency of vision as Orion evolves as an emotionally-aware, memory-integrated, agentic AI system.
